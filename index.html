About Us

Our journey of excellence since 1994

Established in 1994, Samson Holdings began as an offset printers and large format fabric printers for a limited market.

With a clear vision of becoming Sri Lanka's most versatile & leading Import & Product base company, we've grown exponentially. Today, we stand as a testament to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction across multiple sectors including manufacturing, technology, and chemical products.

Our commitment to excellence and continuous improvement has enabled us to expand our portfolio and serve a diverse range of industries. We take pride in our journey and look forward to creating more milestones in the years to come.
